03 · Logo Usage

Clear space, protection.

Give the mark room to breathe. The exclusion zone is measured by the cap-height of the “S” in the wordmark — never less, ideally more.

Clear Space
S
S
S
S
↕ S height
↕ S height
↔ S width
↔ S width

Use the height of the letter “S” as a minimum margin on all four sides of the lockup. In tight applications — favicons, badges, social avatars — the icon-only mark may be used in place of the full lockup.

Minimum Sizes
16 PX · DIGITAL
32 PX · UI
24 PT · PRINT

The icon may shrink to 16 px; the full lockup should not appear smaller than 24 pt in print or 96 px in digital. Below those sizes, switch to the icon-only mark.

Misuses
Don't · Stretch

Never distort the proportions of the mark by scaling on one axis.

Don't · Recolor

Use only the approved palette. No gradients, no off-brand color.

Don't · Bury

Avoid placing the mark on busy backgrounds or low-contrast surfaces.

05 · Typography

Set in two voices.

A high-contrast serif carries identity and presence; a humanist sans handles the everyday work of reading. Together they hold the tone — refined, but never precious.

Aa Bb
Cormorant Garamond
Display · Serif
Light 300 · Regular 400 · Italic

Used for titles, the wordmark, editorial moments and large numbers. Set in light or regular weight, with generous tracking on capitals (0.18–0.22em).

Descriptor · Web & Bio
Operations designed for the company you actually run.

Call to Action
Start with the diagnosis.
Single primary CTA across the site, decks, and proposals. Names the first concrete step — the Diagnostic Sprint — without selling it.
Voice in Practice
✓ How we say it
“Before we design anything, we understand what is actually happening. Then we build something that fits.”
Plain words. Short sentences. We name the work before we name the outcome. No jargon, no hedging, no superlatives.
× How we don't
“We leverage best-in-class methodologies to unlock transformational outcomes for visionary leaders.”
Consulting cliché. We do not perform expertise. We demonstrate it by being specific about the work.
